Symptom Checker

Possible Condition: Viral Infection
Based on your symptoms, you may be experiencing a viral infection. Most viral infections resolve on their own with rest and hydration.
Recommended Precautions:
  • Get plenty of rest
  • Stay hydrated by drinking fluids
  • Take over-the-counter pain relievers if needed
  • Monitor your temperature regularly
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms worsen or persist beyond 3 days

About Community HealthCare

Our Mission

Community HealthCare is a mobile platform designed to provide accessible healthcare information and services to rural and underserved communities. Our goal is to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and those in need through technology.

Mobile-First Healthcare

We deliver essential health information directly to your mobile device, even with limited internet connectivity.

Local Language Support

Our platform is available in multiple regional languages to ensure everyone can access healthcare information.

Preventive Healthcare

We focus on prevention through education, helping communities recognize early warning signs of common health issues.

Key Features

  • Health record tracking for individuals and families
  • Local healthcare facility finder
  • Women's health and menstrual cycle tracking
  • Nutrition and diet recommendations
  • Emergency contact information
